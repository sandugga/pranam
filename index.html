Our Story

A regenerative food ecosystem — reconnecting Earth, Farmer & Family

Somewhere along the way, modern supply chains severed the connection between seed, farmer, food and family. Rising lifestyle diseases are pushing people to ask hard questions about their food. We built PRANAM × G.R.O.W to answer them.

G.R.O.W (Generational Renewal of Wisdom) focuses on indigenous seed conservation, farmer knowledge networks and community seed banks. PRANAM is our marketplace arm — aggregating natural foods with full farmer identity and transparent supply chains.

Every rupee you spend goes towards farmer livelihoods, seed sovereignty and soil regeneration.

Seed Sovereignty

Every product carries a farmer's name. 3-layer verification ensures authenticity. Fair pricing and direct market linkage for farmer livelihoods.

Regenerative Food Systems
